__You can buy ready-cut TetraPlikes below or download the templates:

__1. Place the two unfolded halves of the TetraPlikes with their printed sides facing outwards.
__2. Spin the bottom or top sheet through 90 degrees.
__3. Fold both 90 degrees or to right angle on the centreline towards the other.
__4. They can now be clipped together using the tabs and opposing slits.
__5. Finally, fold over the wings that are sticking out,
_____so that they clip into the other sheet. (Step not shown on video)
__6. Each tab or orange line will fold at a right angle.
